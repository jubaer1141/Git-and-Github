# 🧩 Create GitHub Account

This guide will help you create your **GitHub account**, which is required to store and manage your code online.

---

## 🌐 Step 1: Go to GitHub Website

1. Open your browser
2. Go to:
   👉 https://github.com/

---

## 📝 Step 2: Sign Up

1. Click on **Sign up**

2. Enter your:

   * Email address
   * Password
   * Username

3. Click **Continue**

---

## 🔐 Step 3: Verify Your Account

* GitHub may ask you to verify your email
* Check your email inbox
* Click the verification link

---

## 👤 Step 4: Complete Setup

* Follow the on-screen instructions
* You can skip optional steps if you want

---

## ✅ Step 5: Login

* Go to 👉 https://github.com/login
* Enter your email and password
* Click **Sign in**

---

## 🎉 Done!

You have successfully created your GitHub account.

Now you are ready to create your first repository.

👉 [Create First Repository](first-repo.md)
